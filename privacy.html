Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Altimeter is designed with privacy in mind. We believe your location and sensor data should remain yours. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Data Collection

Altimeter accesses the following data from your device:

  • • Location Data: Your GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude) and altitude to display your current position and elevation.
  • • Device Orientation: Pitch and roll measurements from your device's gyroscope and accelerometer for the inclinometer feature.
  • • Device Information: Basic browser and device information (user agent, screen size) for ensuring compatibility and responsive design.

How We Use Your Data

All data processing happens locally in your browser. Here's how your data is used:

  • • Display Real-Time Information: Your location and sensor data are used solely to display current readings in the app interface.
  • • Map Visualization: Your coordinates are sent to Google Maps API to display your location on the interactive map.
  • • Elevation Lookup: When GPS altitude is unavailable, your coordinates are sent to Google Elevation API to retrieve altitude data.

Data Storage

We do not store or log your data. Here's what this means:

  • • No location history is saved or transmitted to any server
  • • No sensor data is recorded beyond the current browser session
  • • All data exists only in your browser's memory while the app is running
  • • Closing the browser tab immediately clears all sensor and location data
  • • No databases, logs, or persistent storage mechanisms are used

Third-Party Services

Altimeter uses the following third-party services:

Google Maps JavaScript API

Used to display your location on an interactive map. Your coordinates are sent to Google's servers to render the map.

Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Elevation API

Used as a fallback to determine altitude when GPS altitude is unavailable. Your coordinates are sent to Google's servers to retrieve elevation data.

Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Tailwind CSS CDN

Used to load styling framework from a content delivery network. No personal data is transmitted; only standard HTTP request data.

Cookies and Tracking

Altimeter does not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms. We do not track your usage, collect analytics, or use any advertising identifiers. The only data exchanged is what's necessary for the app's core functionality (displaying location, altitude, and sensor data).

Permissions

Altimeter requests the following permissions from your browser:

  • • Location Access: Required to obtain your GPS coordinates and altitude. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time through your browser settings.
  • • Motion & Orientation (iOS 13+): Required to access gyroscope and accelerometer data for the inclinometer. On iOS devices, you must explicitly grant this permission.

You have full control over these permissions and can revoke them at any time. Without these permissions, the app cannot function, but no data is collected or stored if you choose not to grant access.

Data Security

Since all data processing happens locally in your browser and we don't transmit or store any personal data on our servers, there is minimal security risk. The only data transmitted is to Google's APIs (Maps and Elevation) over secure HTTPS connections. We recommend using Altimeter only on trusted devices and networks, especially when location data is sensitive.

Children's Privacy

Altimeter is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • • Access: Since no data is stored, there is no data to access beyond what's displayed in the app in real-time.
  • • Deletion: Simply close the browser tab to clear all data from memory.
  • • Opt-Out: You can stop using the app at any time or revoke permissions through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
